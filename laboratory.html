High Purity Germanium Detector (HPGe)

High-Purity Germanium (HPGe) detectors are the gold standard for high-resolution gamma-ray spectroscopy. Their excellent energy resolution enables precise identification and characterization of radioactive isotopes, making them indispensable tools in nuclear physics research, environmental monitoring, and radiation measurements.

X-Ray Fluorescence (XRF) Spectrometer

XRF spectrometers are widely used for elemental analysis in various fields, including materials science, environmental monitoring, and archaeology. They work by measuring the characteristic X-rays emitted by elements when they are excited by an external X-ray source.

Lanthanum Bromide Scintillation Detector

LaBr3:Ce detector is a high-Performance scintillation detector widely used in gamma-ray spectroscopy for its excellent energy resolution and fast timing characteristics.
